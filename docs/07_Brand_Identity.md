# 1. Brand Purpose

PocketIQ exists to help people build healthier financial habits by making money management simple, intelligent, and enjoyable.

The goal is not only to track expenses but to help users understand their financial behavior and make better decisions every day.

---

# 2. Brand Vision

To become the personal finance companion that users trust every day for understanding, managing, and improving their financial life.

---

# 3. Brand Mission

PocketIQ empowers users by combining intelligent automation, beautiful design, and meaningful financial insights into one seamless experience.

---

# 4. Core Values

### Simplicity

Financial information should be easy to understand.

### Trust

Security and privacy come before convenience.

### Intelligence

The application should help users make better decisions rather than simply display numbers.

### Consistency

Every interaction should feel familiar and predictable.

### Delight

Thoughtful animations and polished interactions should make users enjoy using the app.

---

# 5. Brand Personality

PocketIQ should always feel:

* Modern
* Premium
* Minimal
* Intelligent
* Trustworthy
* Calm
* Confident
* Friendly

It should avoid feeling:

* Cluttered
* Corporate
* Childish
* Overly colorful
* Complex

---

# 6. Tone of Voice

PocketIQ speaks like a knowledgeable friend.

Examples:

Instead of:

"Budget Exceeded."

Use:

"You're close to your monthly budget. Consider slowing down your spending."

Instead of:

"Transaction Saved."

Use:

"Expense added successfully."

The tone should always be supportive, never judgmental.

---

# 7. Logo Direction

The logo should represent:

* Clarity
* Growth
* Finance
* Intelligence
* Motion

Design principles:

* Simple enough to recognize at app icon size.
* Works in light and dark themes.
* Minimal details.
* Rounded geometry.
* Scalable as SVG.

---

# 8. Color Strategy

### Primary Color

Deep Indigo

Purpose:

* Brand identity
* Primary buttons
* Active navigation
* Highlights

### Secondary Color

Electric Cyan

Purpose:

* Interactive elements
* Analytics
* Accent highlights

### Success

Emerald Green

Purpose:

* Budget achieved
* Positive insights
* Savings

### Warning

Amber

Purpose:

* Budget nearing limit
* Important reminders

### Error

Coral Red

Purpose:

* Budget exceeded
* Validation
* Failed operations

### Neutral Palette

Multiple neutral shades should be used for:

* Backgrounds
* Cards
* Text hierarchy
* Borders
* Dividers

---

# 9. Typography

Typography should be modern, highly readable, and optimized for mobile devices.

Guidelines:

* Strong emphasis on numerical values.
* Clear visual hierarchy.
* Consistent spacing.
* Minimal font weight variations.

---

# 10. Iconography

Icons should follow a rounded style.

Preferred style:

* Material Symbols Rounded

Categories should have unique, recognizable icons.

---

# 11. Motion Language

Animations should communicate state changes.

Examples:

* Swipe between accounts.
* Animated balance updates.
* Progress rings.
* Interactive charts.
* Bottom sheet transitions.
* Card elevation on interaction.

Target animation duration:

* Fast: 150ms
* Standard: 250ms
* Complex: 350ms

---

# 12. Illustration Style

Illustrations should be minimal and modern.

Use them only for:

* Empty states
* Onboarding
* Success states
* Error recovery

Avoid unnecessary decorative artwork.

---

# 13. Sound & Haptics

Sound effects are optional.

Haptic feedback should be used sparingly for:

* Successful authentication
* Saving transactions
* Pull to refresh
* Important confirmations

---

# 14. Brand Experience

When users open PocketIQ, they should feel:

* In control
* Relaxed
* Informed
* Secure
* Motivated to manage their finances

The application should reduce financial anxiety rather than increase it.

---

# 15. Brand Statement

PocketIQ is a modern personal finance companion designed for people who want complete visibility into their money without the complexity of traditional finance applications.

Every interaction should reinforce trust, simplicity, and confidence.
